Samwise Gamgee

History

Samwise Gamgee, known as Sam, was a Hobbit of the Shire.

He was a gardener and Frodo Baggins's best friend. He accompanied Frodo to Mount Doom and played an integral part in destroying the One Ring.

After returning to the Shire, he married Rosie Cotton and had two little hobbits named Elanor and Frodo.

Abilities

Courage

Sam invades an Orc tower by himself to save Frodo after Frodo had been taken captive.

Loyalty

Sam is charged by Gandalf to protect Frodo during their journey to Mount Doom. His dedication to keep this promise never waivers during the entire arduous journey.

Humility

Sam consistently refers to Frodo as "Master Frodo" and places himself in the role of a servant. When all looks lost, Sam literally puts Frodo on his back and carries him up the mountain to their final destination.

Selflessness

When the Hobbits have nearly run out of food, Sam gives Frodo a helping and deprives himself of meals.
